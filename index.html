Hair Day

The Hair Day shop offers a range of hair care products that are cruelty-free, sulfate-free, paraben-free, and 100% vegan. They also claim to be dermatologist-tested. They have a variety of products such as custom shampoo, conditioner, hair mask, hair serum, and co-wash. They also have a "Function of Beauty" section that focuses on clean, vegan, and cruelty-free formulas with naturally-derived ingredients and powerful science to meet high-quality and testing standards.
